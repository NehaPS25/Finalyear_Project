Welcome

An incident dashboard on a homepage typically provides a real-time overview of ongoing incidents or events within a system, organization, or project. It displays relevant data and key metrics in an easily digestible format, allowing users to quickly assess the status of incidents and take necessary actions.

The Incident Dashboard on the homepage provides a real-time overview of all active incidents, allowing users to stay informed and respond quickly to ongoing issues. It displays crucial information such as the severity of each incident, affected systems, and the current status of resolution efforts. The dashboard is visually organized with color-coded indicators to highlight the urgency of incidents, making it easy for users to identify high-priority issues. Additionally, it includes a progress tracker to show the resolution status, helping teams monitor how close they are to resolving each problem. By centralizing this information, the dashboard ensures that users can efficiently assess the situation, track incident progress, and take immediate action when needed to minimize disruptions and maintain system stability.
